Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, cooked meats

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the pan: Add butter or oil over medium heat.
  3. Cook: Pour in eggs and cook until beginning to set.
  4. Fill: Add your fillings evenly.
  5. Fold: Fold and cook until done.
  6. Serve: Enjoy hot!
